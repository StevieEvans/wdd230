Battle Ground Chamber of Commerce

This is my home town where the chamber will be based on.

Site Purpose

The Battle Ground Chamber of Commerce serves as a place for local businesses to find resources to help one another. The chamber may help bring smaller businesses into the eyes of other businesses to connect with them. The chamber can assist in promoting these smaller businesses as well as connect all associated businesses with eachother. The site can also be a place where community involvement is encourage as well as provide useful services for the community.

Scenarios

Some examples of why someone would want to visit this site would be to see what charity events are the local businesses involved with. Maybe someone wants to find out how they can better market their own self-owned business. Another examples would someone would visit this site would be to get some information about how the local laws and policies affect their local businesses.
